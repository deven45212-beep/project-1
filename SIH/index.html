Rumtek Monastery

One of the largest and most significant monasteries in Sikkim, Rumtek is the seat of the Karmapa Lama. Its grand prayer hall is a masterpiece of Tibetan Buddhist art.

Pemayangtse Monastery

Founded in the 17th century, Pemayangtse is one of the oldest monasteries in the state. It offers breathtaking views of Mount Kangchenjunga and houses ancient sculptures.

Enchey Monastery

Belonging to the Nyingma order, this 200-year-old monastery is perched on a ridge above Gangtok. Its name means 'the solitary temple' and it's known for its vibrant festivals.

The Sacred Monasteries

Discover the timeless serenity and architectural marvels of Sikkim's most revered spiritual centers.

Rumtek Monastery

The largest monastery in Sikkim and the seat of the Karma Kagyu lineage. A vibrant center for Buddhist studies.

Pemayangtse Monastery

One of the oldest monasteries of the Nyingma order, housing a remarkable seven-tiered wooden structure.

Tashiding Monastery

Perched on a heart-shaped hill, this monastery is considered the most sacred, hosting the famous Bhumchu festival.

Kartok Monastery

It is a Buddhist monastery in Pakyong, a town in the foothills of the Himalayas located in the East Sikkim district of the northeastern Indian state of Sikkim.

Phodong Monastery

It is a significant Buddhist monastery located in Sikkim, India, approximately 28 to 35 kilometers north of Gangtok.

Enchey Monastery

The Enchey Monastery was established in 1909 above Gangtok, the capital city of Sikkim in the Northeastern Indian state. It belongs to the Nyingma order of Vajrayana Buddhism.
